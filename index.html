About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an intensive eight‑week online programme that teaches no‑code AI tools to automate data, content, and knowledge workflows. Participants learn to:

  • Automate documents, spreadsheets, and data tasks
  • Streamline literature reviews & knowledge management
  • Build custom chatbots for projects or teams
  • Create professional visuals & presentations
  • Design and present a hands‑on capstone project
